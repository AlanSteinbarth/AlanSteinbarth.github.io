Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2025

1. Who are we?

This website is operated by Alan Steinbarth, VictoryMind.ai. Contact: contact@victorymind.ai

2. What data do we collect?

  • Anonymous website usage statistics (Google Analytics, if accepted)
  • Contact form data (name, email, message, company, subject)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information) for security and performance

3. How do we use your data?

  • To respond to your inquiries via the contact form
  • To improve the website and analyze traffic (if cookies accepted)
  • To ensure website security and prevent abuse
  • To provide personalized AI consulting services

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies only for analytics (Google Analytics) if you accept them. You can decline analytics cookies at any time. We also use essential cookies for website functionality and security.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (only with your consent)
  • Performance cookies: To optimize loading times and user experience

5. Data sharing and third parties

We do not share your data with third parties, except for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymous data, only with consent)
  • Contact forms: Formspree.io for form processing
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages for website hosting
  • Email services: For responding to your inquiries

6. Data retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Contact form data: 2 years or until you request deletion
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Technical logs: 90 days maximum

7. Your rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: For analytics cookies at any time

8. Security measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure third-party services only

9. Contact and data protection officer

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact:

Email: contact@victorymind.ai
Website: VictoryMind.ai

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
